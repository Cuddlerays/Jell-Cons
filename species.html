About Jell-Cons

Jell-Cons are a anthro styled species that function between a mix of organic and inorganic components! Jell-Cons are robotic in various areas as well as infused with a Jell like substance. They have a water tank attached to their backs that is filled with arctic water to help keep the Jell parts of their bodies solid enough to not melt and can be used in place of various parts of their bodies. A rubberized neck with a socket and extending plug resides on the backs of their necks that they use to recharge their bodies, help other Jell-Cons who are low on energy, or help with power outages.

Jell-Cons reside in a futuristic time period, their city, Jell-Con City, is run by Gold Snow, our Mascot.

Jell-I-Pets are also part of our species but do not require to be listed on the Masterlist unless evolved into an anthro phase for their final stage.
